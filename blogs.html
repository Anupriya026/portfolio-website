My first Hackathon

I had a fantastic time at my first 48-hour hackathon, POLLUX 2023, organized by Celestial Biscuit IGDTUW. Our team, 'WeTried,' along with members Samriddhi Tiwari, Riya Ahlawat, and Prakriti Maurya, made it to the top 15 teams in the UX track out of 800+ participants.

We worked on a UX Case Study for a Web Portal for Reading Technical Blogs and Research Papers by Students of IGDTUW. It was a challenging journey from brainstorming ideas to designing the portal mockup and presenting it through a slide deck. However, with effective communication and perseverance, we delivered a mockup we were proud of. Collaborating with talented and passionate individuals was a great learning experience, and I gained a lot from my team members.Participating in the hackathon reminded me of the power of teamwork and the importance of stepping out of my comfort zone. I'm excited to apply the skills and knowledge I gained to future projects. A big thanks to our mentor, Ms. Pooja Gera, and Celestial Biscuit IGDTUW for this amazing opportunity.
